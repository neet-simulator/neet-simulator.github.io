NEET Simulator

NEET Simulator is a game which brings you to the comfy life of a NEET (as if you weren't living it already).

You need tendies to survive. You need NEETBux to buy tendies.
You can earn NEETBux by bashing your keyboard to bits.
These bits are inserted into the top secret crypto algorithm where they are transformed into Bux.

Make sure you buy yourself some nice things. That will make you happy!

WARNING: IF YOU CAN'T GLUE YOUR KEYBOARD BACK TOGETHER, I AM NOT RESPONSIBLE.
